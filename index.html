Our Commitment

Warranty & Coverage

Workmanship Warranty
All installations carry a workmanship warranty. Any fitting-related issue after installation is resolved at zero extra cost.
Insurance Claimable
All windshield and major glass replacements are fully claimable through your vehicle insurance. We assist with documentation.
ADAS Recalibration Advisory
Camera-integrated windshields require ADAS recalibration after replacement. We advise and assist you in coordination.
Important Notice

Warranty Limitations

Please Read Before Booking

Warranty covers workmanship only — not the glass itself. Coverage is void for damage caused by vandalism, theft, natural disasters, vehicle collisions, or wear and tear.

Used glass parts are supplied as-is with no warranty. By accepting completed service, you confirm the installation has been inspected to your satisfaction.

For vehicles with camera-integrated windshields (ADAS): recalibration must be completed at a certified dealer after replacement. This is a manufacturer requirement and is not performed by us.
